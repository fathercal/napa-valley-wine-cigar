Run by two people, not a company

Twenty-eight years, one shop, two names you'll actually remember.

Napa Valley Wine & Cigar opened in 1997 in a small space in Bel Aire Plaza. By 2008, Randy and Carrie Bowman had grown it enough to move to a dedicated location on Silverado Trail — humidor, cellar, and two lounges included.

Carrie's background in restaurants, bars, and hospitality shows in how the shop runs: everything on the shelf is something she's tasted and stands behind personally. It's still just the two of them running it, which is exactly how they've kept it feeling the way it does.
